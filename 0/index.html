Project 0 — Fun with Filters and Frequencies

Part 1: Selfie: The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

After taking the images, it seems that when the camera is very close, my nose and forehead were much larger relative to other parts of my face/head. Stepping back and zooming makes the face look natural since everything is more equal in distance from the camera.

Part 2: Architectural Perspective Compression

Zooming from far away makes all of the objects look squished together because distances seem smaller. Walking closer changes the perspective so nearby things look much bigger than far ones.
